Shitakiri

Aug 10th, 2014

English: Sometimes some horrible things may happen in stories – I just read about a poor swallow whose tongue was cut by an old woman for eating her nori. Strange enough the swallow can still talk after this.

にほんごで:　したきりをよんでいる。ばあさはすずめのしたをきった。むごいね。でもするとすずめははなすことができます。どうそうする？
