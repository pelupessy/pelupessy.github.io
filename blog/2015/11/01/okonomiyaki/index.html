Okonomiyaki

Nov 1st, 2015

えいごで: Kansai-style okonomiyaki differs from Hiroshima-style in that the latter includes noodles. Both styles of savoury pancake are often backed on the table by the costumers. I have been told that together with monjayaki these are a favourite among Japanese students.

日本語で: けんきゅうしゃといっしょにおこのみやきのたべほうだいにいった。
