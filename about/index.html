My name is Florian Pelupessy. I am corrently a postdoctoral researcher in Mathematics at Tohoku University in Sendai and have joined the University aikido club. The JSPS granted me a postdoctoral fellowship from 2013 until 2015 at the same university.

My research interest is concrete mathematical independence (unprovability) and reverse mathematics. My past research concerned phase transitions in Peano Arithmetic and its fragments.
