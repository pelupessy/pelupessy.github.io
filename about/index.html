About

ぺルぺッシー　フロリャン　です。がくしんのがいこくじん　とくべつ　けんきゅういん　です。せんだいにすんでいます。

My name is Florian Pelupessy. I am a postdoctoral researcher in Mathematics and have joined the Tohoku University aikido club. The JSPS has granted me a Fellowship for a 24 month stay at the Mathematical Institute of Tohoku University in Sendai.

My research interest is concrete mathematical independence (unprovability). My past research concerned phase transitions in Peano Arithmetic and its fragments.
